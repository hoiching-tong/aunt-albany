Our thoughts about Albany Jones

My Favorite Memory with You
Ching Ching Tong

When I think of Aunt Albany, I think of one of my best examples in life. She has always been there for me, and has loved and cared for me like another daughter. She always supported me in all her endeavors and gives all of herself to her family and friends. She is always so trustworthy and I can talk to her about anything for hours. I could not imagine my life without her and all she has done for me. I think anyone would be lucky to have Albany Jones in their life.

What I Love about You
Kiu Kiu Tong

The things I could say about Aunt Albany could fill up an entire novel. I can describe how much she loves me, and every single person in her life. I can tell you of all the times when one of her hugs just made a terrible day become a beautiful one. I could go on and on listing out all the reasons why I cherish her. But when it comes right down to it, Aunt Albany has always been someone I can count on in my life. She loves me unconditionally, and I know that whatever happens, I can always turn to her for comfort. She has been like a 2nd mom to me for my entire life. And for that, I'll be eternally grateful. For that, I'll love her forever.

Who YOU are in my Life
Amanda Wong

I have always thought of Aunt Albany as a second mother. She has been there for me through all the good times and the bad, has counseled me when I needed advice, and has been one the best confidants I've ever had. I feel so lucky to have her in my life.

What I think when I think of You

The first thing that comes to mind is how generous she is with her love. She is, in all the best ways, another mother to me.
